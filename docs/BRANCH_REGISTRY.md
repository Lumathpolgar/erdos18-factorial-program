## Branch rules

- Do not force-push.
- Do not merge directly without review.
- Keep research commits scoped to the assigned track.
- Cross-track dependencies move through versioned handoffs.
- Main-branch theorem status changes require registry updates.
- Failed conjectures and counterexamples remain in history.
