\[
\frac{g_t}{D_t}<1.108
\]

for every blocked carrier layer that had been audited. This was never promoted to an asymptotic theorem.

The overflow-safe exact `n=57` certificate gives at layer `3`

\[
D_3=2{,}911{,}465{,}312{,}076,
\]

\[
g_3=3{,}399{,}069{,}458{,}070,
\]

and therefore

\[
\frac{g_3}{D_3}
=1.1674772300983786306\ldots>1.108.
\]

Hence the numerical candidate bound `g_t/D_t<1.108` is false.
