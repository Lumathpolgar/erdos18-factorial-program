# Nova 1 Construction Registry

## Candidate A: Valuation-band packets

Status: `OPEN`

Partition prime powers available in `n!` into bands by logarithmic size. Build divisor packets from disjoint valuation budgets and record:

- numerical scale;
- number of choices;
- maximum selected terms;
- collision criteria;
- correction compatibility;
- additive theorem required.

## Candidate B: Shared-core marked families

Status: `OPEN_WITH_HISTORICAL_RESTRICTIONS`

Use factorial-specific cores with markers while avoiding the sequential one-choice flat-gap architecture ruled out for the historical `L_m` route. Any version must explain why the Phase 12P information ceiling does not apply.

## Candidate C: Mixed-radix factorial decomposition

Status: `OPEN`

Explore divisor-based mixed-radix decompositions. Every digit contribution must be a distinct numerical divisor of `n!`, and total digit cost must be polylogarithmic.

## Required record for every construction

1. exact divisor formula;
2. valuation budget proof;
3. numerical range;
4. number of available choices;
5. selection rule;
6. distinctness proof;
7. target range;
8. correction mechanism;
9. final term count;
10. known obstruction tests.
