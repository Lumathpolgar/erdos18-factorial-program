# Nova 1 Open Requirements

## Requirements from Nova 2

### N1-REQ-N2-001

Status: `DRAFT`

Given a frozen factorial layer system with total term budget `k=O((log n)^2)`, prove final-window occupancy for every target in `[0,floor(sqrt(n!))]` up to the residual width supplied by Nova 1's correction palette.

The final request will specify supports, selection constraints, lattice span, and residual width.

## Requirements from Nova 3

### N1-REQ-N3-001

Status: `DRAFT`

Provide uniform lower bounds for the number of admissible factorial divisors or valuation profiles in the logarithmic windows used by the candidate atlas.

The final request must state the weakest sufficient window width and target range.

## Requirements from Nova 4

### N1-REQ-N4-001

Status: `DRAFT`

Implement legality, collision, coverage, and term-count tests for each proposed structural construction, and search for the smallest failure.

## Rule

No requirement may be sent until its exact sufficient conclusion is frozen. Stronger, aesthetically preferred statements must be separated from statements actually needed by the proof architecture.
