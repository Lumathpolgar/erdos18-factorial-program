## Exact domain

Every integer

```text
20 <= n <= 80
```

was audited under the exact reduced construction from the handoff. For each `n`, the verifier recomputed:

- `P_n`, the primes in `(n/2,n]`, and the squarefree product `P_n`;
- the exact core menu `H_n={d:d|P_n,1<d<=sqrt(P_n)}`;
- `r_n^*`, `M_n^*`, and all layer addresses;
- every legal layer term and its exact divisibility by `n!`;
- exact target-truncated rainbow support in `[0,T_n]`, `T_n=min(floor(sqrt(n!)),10^7)`;
- formal profile counts and distinct reachable sums;
- gcd and residue occupancy for every modulus `2<=q<=64`;
- maximum downward gap and every record-gap predecessor witness;
- the first target whose downward gap exceeds `2^{r_n^*}-1`.

All arithmetic is exact integer arithmetic. `ceil(log n)` is certified using rational interval bounds.

## Collision diagnostic

The committed table reports formal profile count and distinct reachable sums up to `T_n`. Their ratio is a compression diagnostic, not a pure collision probability, because formal profiles whose total exceeds `T_n` are also absent from the truncated support. The verifier does not promote this diagnostic to a theorem about full-sum collisions.
