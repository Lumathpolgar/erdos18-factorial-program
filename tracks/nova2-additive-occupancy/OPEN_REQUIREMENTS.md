# Nova 2 Open Requirements

## Requirements from Nova 1

### N2-REQ-N1-001

Status: `DRAFT`

Provide two explicit factorial layer systems with:

- exact divisor formulas;
- numerical disjointness;
- support bounds;
- selection budget;
- correction palette and residual radius.

## Requirements from Nova 3

### N2-REQ-N3-001

Status: `DRAFT`

For a frozen probabilistic layer model, provide uniform estimates for moments, maximal atoms, characteristic functions, and local mass in the required target range.

### N2-REQ-N3-002

Status: `DRAFT`

Determine whether the candidate convolution has a nontrivial lattice span or concentration on proper residue classes.

## Requirements from Nova 4

### N2-REQ-N4-001

Status: `DRAFT`

Simulate candidate convolutions exactly for feasible `n`, test every target window, measure collisions and local minima, and search for modular or boundary failures.

## Rule

Every request must distinguish between data needed for a heuristic model and data sufficient for a proof.
