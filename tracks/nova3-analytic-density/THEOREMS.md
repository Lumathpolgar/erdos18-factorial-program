# Nova 3 Theorem Registry

## Active statements

### N3-ANA-001

- Status: `OPEN`
- Goal: Obtain uniform upper and lower bounds for
  \[
  \#\{d\mid n!:e^u\le d\le e^{u+\Delta}\}
  \]
  in the ranges required by accepted structural constructions.

### N3-ANA-002

- Status: `OPEN`
- Goal: Quantify the entropy, mean, covariance, and lattice properties of admissible valuation vectors
  \[
  0\le a_p\le v_p(n!).
  \]

### N3-ANA-003

- Status: `OPEN`
- Goal: Prove characteristic-function or exponential-sum estimates for frozen layer measures supplied by Nova 2.

### N3-OBS-001

- Status: `OPEN`
- Goal: Derive analytic ceilings that identify impossible local-density or maximum-gap requests before they become proof dependencies.

## Promotion requirements

Every result must state its full uniformity range, window width, parameter dependencies, and whether it counts all smooth integers or only actual divisors of `n!`.
