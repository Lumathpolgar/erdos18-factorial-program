# Nova 3 Open Requirements

## Requirements from Nova 1

### N3-REQ-N1-001

Status: `AWAITING_FROZEN_CONSTRUCTION`

Nova 1 must provide exact divisor bands, logarithmic windows, target ranges, and the weakest sufficient lower or upper count.

## Requirements from Nova 2

### N3-REQ-N2-001

Status: `AWAITING_FROZEN_MODEL`

Nova 2 must provide exact layer probability measures and the required moment, maximal-atom, lattice, and characteristic-function estimates.

## Requirements from Nova 4

### N3-REQ-N4-001

Status: `DRAFT`

Compute exact divisor-count histograms and valuation-profile statistics for feasible `n` to test asymptotic formulas and identify transition regimes.

## Self-owned requirements

### N3-SELF-001

Create a scale map for `log d` when `d|n!`, including central scale, half-range boundary, and prime-band entropy contributions.

### N3-SELF-002

Prove at least one nontrivial uniform upper bound for local factorial-divisor counts that can reject impossible density conjectures.

## Rule

Do not solve an undefined stronger problem. Every incoming request must specify the exact estimate needed and why it closes a named theorem dependency.
