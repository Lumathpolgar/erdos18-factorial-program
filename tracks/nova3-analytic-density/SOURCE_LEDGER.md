## Initial research areas

### Factorial divisor asymptotics

Status: `TO_RESEARCH`

Locate rigorous results on the number and distribution of divisors of `n!`, including local or weighted variants.

### Smooth and ultrafriable numbers

Status: `TO_RESEARCH`

Identify which estimates can upper-bound or approximate factorial divisors without incorrectly replacing valuation constraints.

### Saddle-point and local-limit methods

Status: `TO_RESEARCH`

Locate lattice local central limit and saddle-point theorems applicable to bounded independent prime-exponent variables with nonidentical weights.

### Exponential sums and anti-concentration

Status: `TO_RESEARCH`

Locate estimates suitable for characteristic functions of factorial valuation profiles and rainbow layer convolutions.

## Rule

A bibliographic citation alone is not sufficient. The exact theorem statement and compatibility check must be recorded.
