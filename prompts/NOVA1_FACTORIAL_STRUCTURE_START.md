## Frozen mathematical objective

The main repository target is

\[
h(n!) < (\log n)^{O(1)}.
\]

The current sufficient local target is

\[
H_{n!}\!\left(\lfloor\sqrt{n!}\rfloor+1\right)=O((\log n)^2).
\]

Do not silently change endpoint conventions. Use the repository definition of `H_N(X)` exactly.

## Your mission

Exploit the full prime-valuation structure

\[
v_p(n!)=\sum_{j\ge 1}\left\lfloor\frac{n}{p^j}\right\rfloor
\]

to build deterministic divisor families, packets, correction systems, or reductions that could support the factorial half-range theorem with a polylogarithmic number of distinct divisors.

Your work must distinguish factorial-specific advantages from the stronger and partially obstructed lcm route. Do not import an `L_m` argument unless you prove that its hypotheses transfer to `n!`.
